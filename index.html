Our Programs

Kids Art Class

At Moon Studio, we follow a structured module-based art program designed to guide children step by step in developing strong artistic foundations. Students are introduced to drawing fundamentals, explore multiple colour mediums, and engage in creative craft activities that encourage imagination and confidence. Our program also provides an added advantage in developing realistic drawing skills through guided practice and progressive learning.

Learning Levels:

🌱 Beginner
Focus on basic drawing skills, line control, shapes, colouring fundamentals, and simple creative craft exploration.

🌿 Intermediate
Focus on structured drawing, colour techniques, composition, and guided creative projects.

🎨 Advanced
Focus on realism, shading techniques, and refined artistic expression.
